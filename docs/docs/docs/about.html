Our Story

A sweet spot for Manor

We're a locally owned shop serving creative treats and neighborly hospitality. From classic scoops to boba and mangonadas, we make it fresh and love customizing your order.

Every cup, cone, and smile is made right here in Manor, Texas. We believe in quality ingredients, warm service, and being a gathering place for our community.

Thanks for supporting a small business built with love and dedication. We can't wait to serve you!
